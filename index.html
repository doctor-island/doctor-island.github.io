DEREK BEYER

Writer • Civic Technologist • Steadfast Utopian

I'm a writer and civic technologist who explores the intersection of idealism and reality through both fiction and public service. Currently developing Doctor Eiland, a genre-blending novel that uses supervillain tropes to examine utopianism and the moral complexities of trying to change the world.

My work spans from helping governments translate complex processes into accessible language at Bloom Works, to curating speculative fiction that grapples with political and social questions. I'm particularly fascinated by mid-century scientist-intellectuals, science fiction as cultural mythology, and how vividly imagining better futures can help bring them into existence.

Based in Ann Arbor, Michigan, I bring both my perspective as an autistic person and my expertise in content strategy to exploring what it means to be human in an age of technological change.
